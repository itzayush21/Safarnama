Police Bazar, Shillong

Description: The most bustling market in Shillong, it's a hub for tourists and locals alike.

Best Purchases: Handicrafts, traditional Khasi clothes, and local produce. It's also an excellent place to sample local foods and sweets.

Bara Bazar (Lewduh Market), Shillong

Description: One of the oldest and largest traditional markets in the northeast, primarily run by local Khasi women.

Best Purchases: Handwoven shawls, bamboo handicrafts, traditional jewelry, and spices. You can also find fresh local produce, meats, and traditional fermented foods. It's a great place to experience local culture.

Iew Duh Market, Shillong

Description: A traditional local market where you can witness the day-to-day life of the locals.

Best Purchases: Fermented fish, local fruits and vegetables, and traditional Khasi garments. This market gives a glimpse of the local way of life, making it more than just a shopping experience.

Handicrafts Emporium, Shillong

Description: Managed by the state's Directorate of Handicrafts and Handlooms, this emporium showcases the rich arts and crafts of Meghalaya.

Best Purchases: Cane and bamboo crafts, handwoven textiles, and wooden artifacts. This is the ideal place to buy authentic Meghalayan handicrafts.
