Welcome to Manipur Tourism

Day 1: Imphal - The Cultural Capital of Manipur

Your adventure begins in Imphal, the vibrant cultural capital of Manipur. Explore the historic Kangla Fort, visit the serene Shri Govindajee Temple, and stroll through the colorful Ima Keithel (Mother's Market).

Explore Imphal: Watch Video 1 | Watch Video 2

Day 2: Loktak Lake - The Floating Wonderland

Travel to Loktak Lake, one of the largest freshwater lakes in Northeast India. Take a boat ride to the unique floating islands, Phumdis, and witness the serene beauty of this natural wonder.

Explore Loktak Lake: Watch Video 1 | Watch Video 2

Day 3: Keibul Lamjao National Park - Home of the Brow-Antlered Deer

Visit Keibul Lamjao National Park, the world's only floating national park, where you can spot the endangered Brow-Antlered Deer, also known as the Sangai, in their natural habitat.

Explore keibul Lamjao National park: Watch Video

Day 4: Excursion to Moirang - The Flagbearer of Freedom

Explore Moirang, a town steeped in history and known for its pivotal role in India's struggle for independence. Visit the INA Memorial Complex and pay tribute to the heroes of the Azad Hind Fauj.

Explore Excursion to Moirang-The Flagbearer of Freedom: Watch Video

Day 5: Khongjom - Reliving a Historic Battle

Journey to Khongjom, where you'll step back in time to the Battle of Khongjom, a significant event in Manipur's history. Visit the Khongjom War Memorial and Museum to gain insights into the valiant struggle.
